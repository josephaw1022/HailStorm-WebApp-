What is the Hailstorm Problem?

Take any number (literally any positive integer) : 23 , 4534 , 32 , 12 , 32, etc...

if X (the number) is even then, new X = (old X)/2

else, new X = 3*(old X) +1

.... If you keep going for any number, you will always reach 1

Examples

15 -> 46 -> 23 -> 70 -> 35 -> 106 -> 53 -> 160 -> 80 -> 40 -> 20 -> 10 -> 5 -> 16 -> 8 -> 4 -> 2 ->1

34 -> 17 -> 52 -> 26 -> 13 -> 40 -> 20 -> 10 -> 5 -> 16 -> 8 -> 4 -> 2 ->1
